FAQ Section

How do I file a complaint?
Simply click "File Complaint", fill out the form with your issue details, upload any supporting photos, and submit. You'll receive a unique tracking ID immediately.
What types of complaints are accepted?
We accept complaints about products, services, civic issues, government departments, consumer rights violations, and municipal services.
How long does resolution take?
Average resolution time is 15 days, though it varies by complaint type and complexity. You can track progress in real-time with your unique ID.
Is this service free?
Yes, filing and tracking complaints is completely free. There are no hidden charges or fees for using our platform.
